# Sketch Manifest

## Design Direction

Designer-grade portfolio for Caleb Lim — cross-functional analyst + brand-management generalist. Splash-picker UX: "What do you wish to see?" routes recruiters into one of four discipline rooms (Graphic Design, Financial Models, Personal Projects, Marketing). The site itself doubles as a brand artifact. Aesthetic must NOT read as AI-generated; references are Readymag and Framer.

## Reference Points

- Readymag editorial templates (magazine-grade typography, asymmetric grids, restrained motion)
- Framer template showcases (color-blocked maximalism, scroll-driven moments, rotated/skewed elements)
- Anti-references: shadcn cards, centered-hero gradients, Inter-only typography, lucide icons, bento grids, "Built with X" footers — actively avoided

## Locked Design Anchor (from sketch 001)

- **Direction:** Magazine maximalist
- **Type pairing:** *Bricolage Grotesque* (display, set huge + tight) + *Fraunces* italic (editorial accent) + *JetBrains Mono* (micro-labels)
- **Color system:** Warm cream paper (#f4ebd9) + ink black + 4 saturated brand colors (terracotta `#e85d2a`, cobalt `#1947ff`, electric lime `#d4ff3a`, plum `#5a1a55`); category page inverts to ink-black background
- **Layout language:** Color-blocked rounded cards with subtle rotation (-1° to +1°), layered decorative elements (large outline circles, italic numerals, dotted lines, triangles), pulsing status pill, "→ PICK ONE" sticker tags
- **Splash structure:** Topbar → Hero band (portrait placeholder · name+roles · bio block) → "What do you wish to see?" question bar → 4 discipline cards (visible above fold) → footer
- **Anti-AI tells actively rejected:** No centered hero, no shadcn cards, no Inter, no lucide icons, no bento grid, no purple gradients, no "Built with X" footer
