# Sketch Manifest

## Design Direction

Designer-grade portfolio for Caleb Lim — cross-functional analyst + brand-management generalist. Splash-picker UX: "What do you wish to see?" routes recruiters into one of four discipline rooms (Graphic Design, Financial Models, Personal Projects, Marketing). The site itself doubles as a brand artifact. Aesthetic must NOT read as AI-generated; references are Readymag and Framer.

## Reference Points

- Readymag editorial templates (magazine-grade typography, asymmetric grids, restrained motion)
- Framer template showcases (color-blocked maximalism, scroll-driven moments, rotated/skewed elements)
- Anti-references: shadcn cards, centered-hero gradients, Inter-only typography, lucide icons, bento grids, "Built with X" footers — actively avoided
